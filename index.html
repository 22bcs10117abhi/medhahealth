fragmented, lost, and invisible.

Every hospital visit starts from zero. Doctors make decisions without context. Patients repeat the same story — again and again.

What We're Building

A new layer of
health intelligence for India.

We're not building another health app. We're building the infrastructure layer that makes every hospital smarter the moment a patient walks in.

01
One card. Every hospital.
A physical smart card that carries your health identity — tap it at any connected facility and your complete medical context is available to your care team, instantly. No phone required. Works without internet.
02
AI that understands your history
Our clinical AI processes years of medical records and surfaces what matters most for the current visit — conditions, medications, alerts — in a format doctors can act on in seconds, not hours.
03
Built for India's health ecosystem
Fully compliant with ABDM and FHIR R4 standards. Designed to work with India's existing hospital management systems from day one — no rip-and-replace required.
04
Health benefits that can't be stolen
A programmable wallet that routes government health subsidies directly to verified facilities via smart contracts — eliminating the leakage, fraud, and delays that cost patients and the state every year.
